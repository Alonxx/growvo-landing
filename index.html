Four minutes a day

Small reps for the moments that change the room.

Growvo is not a passive course library or a motivational feed. Each session asks you to make a real move under constraint, then gives you a principle you can actually remember later.

  • Answer clearly without rushing.
  • Handle heat without collapsing your position.
  • Recover from mistakes before the room defines them.
